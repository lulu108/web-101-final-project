Honey Bees

Aween Karim

Honeybees are social insects and just like most insects, they live in a group which is called a hive. The bees build their hives in hexagonal (six-sides) shaped cells and they use wax to build them. Hundreds of these cells are called honeycombs. There are three types of bees within every hive, one queen bee, male bees known as drones, and female bees known as workers.
